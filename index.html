Razia’s Ray of Hope Foundation is a non-profit organization that provides Afghan girls nurturing, inspiring educational environments and essential resources and services to girls, women and their families.

Current Initiatives

Educating Girls, Removing Barriers to Education, and Providing Humanitarian Aid

Enrollment Expansion

The Enrollment Expansion program has added additional classes at all K-6th grade levels. RRH’s enrollment in grades K-6 has increased 60% since the Taliban’s return in 2021, and the wait list continues to grow.

Nutrition Program

Afghanistan is experiencing one of the worst food crises in the world. RRH’s Nutrition Program provides meals and snacks to girls in school, take-home food staples and cooking supplies.

Menstrual & Hygiene Health

RRH’s Menstrual Health and Hygiene Program distributes hygiene essentials to over 2,000 girls and women, with provisions of menstrual products, laundry and body soap, shampoo, and new underwear.

Educating Afghan girls is the best hope for a better, brighter future for Afghanistan and our world.
